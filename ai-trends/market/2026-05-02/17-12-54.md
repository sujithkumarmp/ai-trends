### Why This Strategy  
The Bull Call credit spread offers protection from volatility and an expected rangebound market to capitalize on bullish sentiment while collecting premiums. The trade is a relatively safe option with low risk even during significant moves in the stock price. 



### Risk Management
 * **Entry Price Risk:** If NVDA trades closer to $185, there is profit opportunity.
 * **Exit Point:** Limit orders are important on this type of strategy; if market direction changes as expected, exit your positions accordingly and make sure you have a pre-determined stop-loss order in place.  


### Alternative Scenario
 If the options market moves against me, I will: move to short calls spread or tighten credit spreads and adjust breakevens after analyzing new price action trends and IV changes.
